Design a premium futuristic bilingual portfolio website for Ahmed Helal, a visual designer. The website should feel cinematic, high-end, immersive, and strategically minimal, combining a zero-gravity space atmosphere with clean editorial precision.

Core objective:
Create a full website design direction, not just a single hero section. The website is for a visual designer, so it must feel like a creative brand experience, not a generic SaaS landing page.

Overall mood and style:

- Dark futuristic visual system with premium black surfaces
- Strong white typography with sharp contrast
- Accent glow lighting inspired by deep violet, electric blue, and subtle orange where needed
- Space-inspired atmosphere with particles, orbital motion, ambient haze, nebula glow, thin luminous lines, and floating interface fragments
- Clean visual hierarchy and strong negative space
- Glassmorphism used carefully, not excessively
- Feels expensive, interactive, modern, and design-driven
- Mix of cinematic motion-site energy and structured portfolio clarity
- The design should feel like a fusion of a motion-driven agency website and a high-end interactive designer portfolio

Website structure:

1. Intro cinematic hero section
2. 3D laptop core section directly after the hero
3. Featured work / portfolio gateway
4. Services universe section
5. Client logos marquee
6. Process / how I work section
7. Testimonials / social proof
8. Metrics / highlights
9. AI assistant callout layer
10. Contact / WhatsApp conversion CTA
11. Premium footer

Very important:

- The intro hero is not a replacement for the laptop
- The laptop section is a core signature element of the experience
- The hero should create emotional impact, then transition into the laptop section naturally
- The website must support both English and Arabic from the beginning
- The design must work equally well for desktop and mobile
- Avoid generic SaaS layouts and generic startup visuals
- Avoid overusing purple in a childish way; keep it mature and premium
- Avoid clutter
- Avoid template-like sections
- Avoid flat boring cards

Section details:

1. Intro Hero
   A cinematic opening section with strong typography, layered cosmic atmosphere, elegant floating UI hints, and motion-driven depth. This section introduces Ahmed Helal as a visual designer with a concise but powerful value proposition. Include CTA buttons like View Portfolio and Start a Project. It should feel bold, premium, and memorable.

2. 3D Laptop Section
   Immediately after the intro hero, present a signature 3D laptop experience. The laptop should feel refined, dimensional, and premium. Inside the laptop screen, the interface should support:

- home/profile state
- portfolio viewer state
- CV viewer state
  The section should feel interactive and central to the brand experience.

3. Featured Work
   A curated work gateway that presents categories or featured visual directions such as branding, sports design, campaign visuals, social media design, and print/packaging. This should feel elegant and art-directed.

4. Services Universe
   A more immersive services section, potentially using orbital or connected-node logic, followed by detailed floating service cards.

5. Client Logos Marquee
   A premium scrolling trust strip placed between services and testimonials. It should feel subtle, elegant, and integrated into the visual system.

6. Process Section
   A clean but creative explanation of how Ahmed works, showing strategic thinking and design process.

7. Testimonials
   Social proof cards with a premium futuristic treatment. Strong readability, elegant spacing, and subtle motion.

8. Metrics
   Confident numeric highlights that reinforce experience and trust.

9. AI Assistant Layer
   A visually integrated callout for a smart AI assistant that helps visitors ask about services, style, and project fit.

10. Contact CTA
    A strong final conversion section focused mainly on WhatsApp, with premium CTA treatment.

11. Footer
    Minimal, elegant, branded, and polished.

Design references and inspiration:
The visual language should sit somewhere between:

- futuristic motion websites
- cinematic portfolio experiences
- premium digital agency websites
- space-inspired minimal interfaces
  The result should be original and brand-led, not derivative.

Important design principles:

- Premium, not playful
- Futuristic, not sci-fi cliché
- Strategic, not decorative chaos
- Motion-aware, not messy
- High contrast, not dull
- Editorial clarity, not UI overload

Output request:
Create a complete website design direction with all major sections visually coherent as one system. Focus on composition, hierarchy, spacing, atmosphere, and the relationship between sections. The design should feel ready to become a production-quality interactive portfolio website.
